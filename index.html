Tommy A. Caruso

Software Engineering Manager · Louisville, KY · github.com/tommy2118

I build primarily in Ruby and Rails. I care about outcomes: clear intent, maintainable design, systems that get cheaper to change over time. I lead engineering teams and show up as a right hand when leaders need one—spot bottlenecks before they become fires, leave things calmer than I found them.

My Air Force background shaped how I work: deliberate over fast, judgment over instinct, train how you fight. The through-line across everything here is reproducible craft—knowledge that survives transmission, speed earned through doctrine, not stolen from quality.

The building and the writing are the same impulse. Different substrates.
